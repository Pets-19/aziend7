VAT Essentials Framework

Complete guide to UAE VAT compliance requirements and best practices

Registration Process

  • Eligibility assessment and threshold analysis
  • EmaraTax platform setup and TRN application
  • Tax Registration Number (TRN) issuance
  • VAT certificate and compliance setup

Filing Requirements

  • Monthly returns (28th of following month)
  • Quarterly submissions for eligible businesses
  • Annual reconciliation and summary filing
  • Digital submission through EmaraTax

Rate Structure

Critical
  • 5% standard rate on most supplies
  • 0% rate for exports and healthcare/education
  • Exempt supplies (residential property, financial)
  • Out-of-scope supplies and place of supply rules

Input/Output Tax

  • Input tax credit mechanism and recovery
  • Output tax calculation and collection
  • Refund procedures and claim optimization
  • Payment obligations and due dates

Record Keeping

  • 5-year record retention requirements
  • Digital compliance and audit trail maintenance
  • Invoice and supporting document management
  • FTA inspection readiness and documentation

Invoice Compliance

  • Tax invoice mandatory elements and format
  • Simplified invoices for B2C transactions
  • Electronic invoicing system integration
  • Credit and debit note procedures
