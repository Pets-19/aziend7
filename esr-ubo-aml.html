ESR (Economic Substance Regulation) Framework

Comprehensive guide to UAE Economic Substance Regulation compliance

ESR Notification

  • Mandatory for all UAE entities
  • Annual filing by June 30
  • Activities declaration required
  • AED 20,000 penalty for non-compliance

Relevant Activities

High Impact
  • • IP Holding Business
  • • Banking Business
  • • Insurance Business
  • • Fund Management
  • • Financing & Leasing
  • • Shipping Business
  • • Headquarters Business
  • • Distribution & Service Centre

Substance Requirements

  • Adequate employees in UAE
  • Adequate expenditure incurred in UAE
  • Core income-generating activities
  • Physical assets in UAE

ESR Report

  • Required for relevant activities
  • Due 12 months after financial year-end
  • Detailed substance information
  • Supporting documentation required

Penalties & Consequences

  • AED 20,000 - 300,000 fines
  • Potential entity dissolution
  • Information exchange with foreign authorities
  • Reputational damage

Compliance Calendar

  • June 30: ESR Notification deadline
  • December 31: ESR Report deadline
  • Ongoing: Substance monitoring
  • Annual: Compliance review

UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) Registry

Complete guide to UAE Ultimate Beneficial Owner identification and registration

UBO Definition

  • 25%+ direct or indirect ownership
  • Control through voting rights
  • Control through other means
  • Nominee arrangements excluded

Registration Timeline

Critical
  • Within 15 days of entity formation
  • Within 15 days of UBO change
  • Annual confirmation required
  • Continuous record maintenance

Required Information

  • Full personal details
  • Ownership percentages
  • Control mechanisms
  • Supporting documentation
