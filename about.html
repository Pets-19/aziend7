Our Story

Founded on Vision

Established in 2009, Aziend was born from a vision to bridge the gap between global business practices and UAE's unique regulatory landscape. Our founders recognized the need for a consultancy that truly understood both worlds.

UAE Market Expertise

Over the past 15 years, we've witnessed and guided businesses through UAE's remarkable transformation. From the introduction of VAT to the recent Corporate Tax reforms, we've been at the forefront of every major change.

Growth & Evolution

What started as a boutique consulting firm has evolved into the UAE's premier business transformation partner, serving everyone from ambitious startups to Fortune 500 enterprises establishing their Middle East presence.
