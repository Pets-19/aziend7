Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Aziend collects, uses, and protects your personal information in compliance with UAE data protection laws.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when using our UAE business consultancy services:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, address)
  • Business information (company details, industry, services needed)
  • Financial information for compliance and regulatory purposes
  • Website usage data (cookies, analytics, preferences)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide our services and meet legal requirements:

  • Delivering UAE business setup and compliance services
  • Processing company formation and banking applications
  • Meeting regulatory compliance requirements (VAT, Corporate Tax, ESR)
  • Communicating about our services and sending updates
  • Improving our website and service offerings

When We Share Information

We only share your information when necessary and with your consent:

  • UAE government authorities for licensing and compliance
  • Banks and financial institutions for account opening
  • Professional service providers (lawyers, auditors) when needed
  • When required by UAE law or legal processes

Note: We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We protect your information using industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmissions
  • Secure data storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on data protection practices
