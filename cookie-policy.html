Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Aziend uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and provide better services.

3-minute read
Manageable Preferences
Privacy Focused

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Improving website functionality and performance
  • Providing relevant content and service recommendations

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality. Cannot be disabled.

  • • Session management
  • • Security features
  • • Form submissions

Functional Cookies

Remember your preferences and personalize your experience.

  • • Language preferences
  • • Contact form data
  • • User interface settings

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand website usage and improve our services.

  • • Page views and traffic
  • • User behavior patterns
  • • Performance metrics

Marketing Cookies

Enable personalized content and relevant service recommendations.

  • • Service recommendations
  • • Content personalization
  • • Communication preferences

Cookie Duration and Storage

Cookie Type Duration Storage
Essential Session only Browser memory
Functional 12 months Local storage
Analytics 24 months Local storage
Marketing 12 months Local storage

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have full control over how cookies are used on our website:

Cookie Banner

Accept or decline non-essential cookies when you first visit

Browser Settings

Configure cookie settings directly in your browser preferences

Private Browsing

Use incognito/private mode to browse without saving cookies

Clear Cookies

Delete existing cookies from your browser at any time

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Tailwind CSS: Content delivery network for styling frameworks
  • Lucide Icons: Icon library for enhanced user interface
  • Font Services: Web font delivery for consistent typography

These services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices. We recommend reviewing their policies for complete information.
